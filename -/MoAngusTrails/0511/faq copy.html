Frequently Asked Question

I can’t see the catalog why won’t it show up on my screen?

In order to load the catalog for viewing you must have the most current version of Adobe Flash Player loaded on your computer. You can obtain this by clicking on the short cut for Adobe Flash Player located in the bottom left of the menu bar.

I can’t get the pdf’s to download.

In order to load the pdf’s for a catalog you must have the most current version of Adobe Acrobat Reader loaded on your computer. You can obtain this by clicking on the short cut for Adobe Acrobat Reader located in the bottom left of the menu bar.


How can I get a closer look and print the catalog?

By clicking on the center of the page a larger clearer file will appear of that particular page for your viewing. From the page you can zoom, print, search for certain words and select text.
