Missouri Angus News
by Brent Murphy, President
I would like to thank our delegates for representing Missouri in
Louisville last month. I hope everyone who attended had a good time.
I can’t believe another calendar year is quickly coming to a close.
Fall breeding is underway and this is always an exciting time of year
at our house and it also happens to be one of the most important parts
of our operation. These decisions will impact our herd for months
and years to come.
The cattle market is good and the weather has been favorable
since most of us got some rain. We as cattle producers have many
things to be thankful for so remember to enjoy everyday we get to
pursue our goals in the cattle business. We are truly lucky to have the
opportunities we have in this country. Good luck to everyone with
their winter work. Please remember to enjoy what you do and do what
you enjoy.
As we look forward to Christmas, my family would like to wish
everyone a very enjoyable holiday season with loved ones and friends.
Please remember the true reason for the season which is the birth of
our Savior. Merry Christmas!
On behalf of the Missouri Angus Association and its mem-
bership our sincere thanks to the elected delegates who participated
in the 128th Annual Meeting of the American Angus Association.
Your time and dedication is greatly appreciated and the respect and
confdence that your fellow members placed in you is honorable. We
appreciate your efforts and sacrifces to attend the meeting. Please
join us all in thank these delegates for their service.
